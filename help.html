1. Headings

Use # symbols to create headings. Number of #s equals heading level.

# H1 Heading
## H2 Heading
### H3 Heading
#### H4 Heading
##### H5 Heading
###### H6 Heading

3. Emphasis

Use * or _ for italics, double for bold, and triple for bold italics.

*italic* or _italic_
**bold** or __bold__
***bold italic*** or ___bold italic___

italic or italic
bold or bold
bold italic or bold italic
